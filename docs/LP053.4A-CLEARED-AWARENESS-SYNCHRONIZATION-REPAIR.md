# LP053.4A — Cleared Awareness Synchronization Repair

## Observed contradiction
After clearing a crossing report and a road-hazard report, Alerts could correctly show the empty state while the Awareness Brief and Travel Brief still presented active community-condition language such as “Several conditions may affect travel,” “Road hazard affecting nearby roads,” and “Crossing delays reported nearby.”

## Root cause
Alerts use lifecycle-correct alert rows and active-count filtering. The awareness story path also filtered active records, but it blended the previous DOM text from the top Awareness Brief back into the next story build. When current active community records were empty, the old top-status text was treated as fresh evidence and could re-elevate the Community Pulse, top Awareness Brief, mobile mirror, and Travel Brief story connection.

## Alerts versus Awareness Brief data path
Alerts derive from the active alert presentation snapshot and lifecycle active rows. Awareness surfaces derive from lightweight active awareness, Community Pulse, the awareness story, and Travel Brief model. LP053.4A keeps those surfaces on lifecycle-correct active community records and prevents previous top-status DOM copy from being re-read as active evidence.

## Clear refresh sequence
A clear write is followed by `loadSharedReports()`, lifecycle filtering, active collection updates, `refreshReportHazardViews()`, Community Pulse shared-model refresh, Travel Brief rebuild, top Awareness Brief rewrite, and mobile mirror synchronization. The repair preserves that sequence and makes the final rebuild authoritative when the active community source is empty.

## Active versus recently cleared semantics
Recently cleared evidence may remain available for cleared-only visibility where current product policy supports it. It must not be counted as an active community travel impact and must not produce active road-hazard, crossing-delay, or “several conditions” wording.

## Repair point
The smallest safe repair is in the active-awareness selection/story path: build the story from lifecycle-correct active community records and explicit story inputs only, not stale Awareness Brief DOM text. Travel Brief Community rows continue to render from `gridlyStoryActiveRecords()`.

## Source separation
Community clearing only resets community contributions. Official Roadways remain based on DriveTexas authority, and Weather remains based on weather authority. Top status may stay elevated if official or weather evidence independently supports an active impact.

## Race-condition handling
The corrected rebuild is idempotent: older DOM copy cannot overwrite a newer quiet active-source snapshot because stale top-status DOM text is no longer an input to the awareness story classifier.

## Duplicate clear investigation
The observed duplicate crossing clear console messages were traced as diagnostics/logging risk, not evidence of a second persisted write in this repair scope. The LP053.4A audit reports `duplicateClearWriteDetected: false` and no event-wiring changes were made.
