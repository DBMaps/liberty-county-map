# Gridly App Screenshot Captions — V277.3

## Objective

Provide suggested caption copy for future App Store and Google Play screenshots. Captions are guidance only; no screenshots are created in V277.3.

Caption tone must preserve Gridly's position as an **Awareness Platform First** and **Route Intelligence Second** product. Avoid claims of official verification, emergency dispatch, turn-by-turn navigation, guaranteed safety, or guaranteed travel times.

## Screenshot 1 — Awareness Brief

**Purpose:** Lead with fast local awareness before users leave.

**Suggested App Store caption:**

Know before you go with a quick local awareness brief.

**Suggested Google Play caption:**

See nearby awareness signals before you head out.

**Review notes:**

- Keep the awareness brief visible and readable.
- Avoid implying official alerts or emergency monitoring.
- Use calm local context rather than fear-based language.
