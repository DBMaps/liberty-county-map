## Long Description

Gridly is built for local awareness.

Before you leave, Gridly helps you understand community-reported conditions that may affect your plans. Check awareness briefs, view reported train-blocked crossings, review road hazards, watch routes, search destinations, and follow the pulse of nearby community activity.

Gridly is designed around a simple idea: know before you go. The app gives you awareness signals and route intelligence so you can better understand what others are seeing in your area.

What Gridly can help with:

- Awareness briefs for local conditions.
- Community reports for crossings, hazards, closures, and related events.
- Route Watch context for places and routes you care about.
- Community Pulse signals that show recent local activity.
- Destination search for checking nearby awareness before heading out.
- Settings that keep the experience focused and lightweight.

Gridly is an awareness platform, not a turn-by-turn navigation app. Reports are community-generated, conditions can change quickly, and official signs, emergency instructions, road closures, and local authorities should always take priority.

Use Gridly before you travel, not while driving. If you need to interact with the app, do so only when it is safe and lawful.
