# GRIDLY V903 END-TO-END BETA EXPERIENCE CERTIFICATION

## Status

**Recommendation: GO WITH OBSERVATIONS**

V903 is an audit/documentation-only milestone for controlled Mobile Portrait beta expansion. It does not modify runtime behavior and does not certify public launch readiness.

## Purpose

V903 certifies that the current Gridly beta experience has been reviewed as an end-to-end tester journey from QR discovery through feedback submission. The certification records the intended beta posture, platform gates, known limitations, protected system boundaries, and required human validation checklist for the next controlled beta expansion decision.

## Scope

This certification covers the documented tester journey for the current beta URL and the supported Mobile Portrait beta experience. It reviews clarity, trust, fit, installation, onboarding, awareness comprehension, map utility, alerts, reports, Community Pulse, Settings, limitations, support, and feedback readiness.

This milestone is documentation-only. It does not introduce application code, runtime configuration, behavioral changes, feature changes, data changes, or distribution automation.

## Mission Alignment

Gridly's mission is: **Know Before You Go**.

V903 supports that mission by confirming that a beta tester can be guided through the awareness-first experience before making travel or situational decisions. The reviewed journey emphasizes understanding conditions, limitations, disclaimers, community signals, and feedback paths before broader use.

## Product Philosophy Alignment

Gridly remains:

1. **Awareness Platform First**
2. **Route Intelligence Second**

V903 certifies the beta experience only within that philosophy. Awareness Brief, Alerts, map context, Community Pulse, known limitations, and feedback loops remain the primary beta value. Route intelligence remains secondary and must not be represented as emergency guidance, official routing, or guaranteed safe passage.

## Supported Platform Statement

The current supported beta experience is **Mobile Portrait only**.

V903 supports controlled Mobile Portrait beta expansion only. Testers should use the beta from a phone in portrait orientation unless future milestones explicitly expand support.

## Gated Platform Statement

Desktop remains gated.

Landscape remains gated.

V903 does not ungate desktop. V903 does not ungate landscape.

## Certification Findings

- The supported beta posture is clear: Mobile Portrait only.
- Desktop and landscape remain intentionally gated.
- The official beta URL is identified for QR and direct access.
- The reviewed journey is appropriate for controlled beta expansion when paired with human validation.
- The beta framing remains aligned with awareness-first product philosophy.
- The journey does not represent Gridly as an emergency-service system.
- The journey does not represent Gridly as an official-source replacement.
- The journey preserves the need for disclaimers, known limitations, and feedback routing.
- Prior beta foundation milestones provide sufficient documentation scaffolding for a controlled beta expansion decision.

## Risks and Observations

- Human device validation is still required before expansion because Mobile Portrait install behavior can differ across iOS, Android, browsers, and home-screen launch paths.
- QR scan validation is still required on representative devices to confirm the official beta URL opens cleanly.
- First-run onboarding, location permission handling, and awareness-area selection remain high-impact moments for tester confidence.
- Alert, reporting, and Community Pulse language must continue to avoid implying official verification or emergency-service readiness.
- Performance perception should be checked on real mobile devices rather than inferred from desktop tooling.
- Known limitations must remain visible enough that testers understand the controlled beta posture.
- Feedback collection must remain easy to find so tester observations can drive the next milestone.

## Required Human Validation Checklist

Before merge or beta expansion, a human reviewer should validate:

- Scan the beta QR code on an iPhone.
- Scan the beta QR code on an Android phone.
- Confirm both QR scans open https://dbmaps.github.io/liberty-county-map/.
- Open the beta URL directly on a mobile phone.
- Confirm the experience remains Mobile Portrait only.
- Confirm desktop remains gated.
- Confirm landscape remains gated.
- Install / add Gridly to the home screen on iOS where available.
- Install / add Gridly to the home screen on Android where available.
- Launch Gridly from the home-screen entry.
- Complete first-run onboarding.
- Select an awareness area.
- Test both allowing and denying location where feasible.
- Review the Awareness Brief for clarity and appropriate disclaimers.
- Inspect the map for mobile portrait fit and usefulness.
- Open Alerts and confirm trust language remains appropriate.
- Submit a test report only in an approved beta/testing manner.
- Review Community Pulse for clarity and non-official-source framing.
- Open Settings and confirm tester-facing clarity.
- Locate known limitations.
- Send or stage feedback through the documented feedback path.
- Confirm no desktop or landscape support claims are introduced.
- Confirm no public-launch, emergency-service, or official-source reliability claims are introduced.
