# Gridly Beta Feedback Guide

Thank you for helping test Gridly. The most helpful feedback is simple, specific, and based on what you saw.

## Questions To Answer

- What confused you?
- What felt slow?
- What felt useful?
- What wording did not make sense?
- Did you trust what Gridly showed you? Why or why not?
- Did you know what to do next?
- Did anything look broken, cut off, or encoded incorrectly?
- Did buttons, menus, and Settings labels look clean?

## Device And Browser Details

Please include:

- Phone, tablet, or computer model if you know it.
- Browser name, such as Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox.
- Whether you opened Gridly in the browser or installed it to your home screen.
- Whether location access was allowed, blocked, or not requested.

## Helpful Examples

Helpful feedback sounds like:

- "I saw a report, but I did not understand whether it was still current."
- "The map loaded, but the report details felt hard to read on my phone."
- "Settings was clear, but I could not find how to replay the walkthrough."
- "I trusted the report because it showed recent activity."

## Safety Reminder

Gridly is in beta and is not for emergencies. For emergencies, always call 911 or local emergency services.
