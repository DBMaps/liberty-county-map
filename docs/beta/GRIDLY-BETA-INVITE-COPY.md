# Gridly Beta Invite Copy

## Short Text Message

I am helping test Gridly, a local awareness app built around "Know Before You Go." It is a controlled beta, and feedback is helpful. Want to try it and tell us what feels clear, useful, or confusing?

## Longer Facebook Or Message Version

Gridly is opening a controlled beta for more local testers. It is a community awareness app designed to help people "Know Before You Go" by checking nearby reports about blocked crossings, road hazards, flooding, closures, and changing travel conditions.

It is still in beta, so feedback matters. We are looking for what feels useful, what feels confusing, what looks broken, and whether the app helps you understand what to do next. Gridly is not for emergencies; always call 911 or local emergency services for emergencies.

## Family And Friends Version

I would like your help testing Gridly. Please use it like you normally would before heading out, then tell us what was clear, what was confusing, and whether anything looked broken. This is a controlled beta, so honest feedback is the goal.

## Local Community Version

We are inviting a small group of local testers to try Gridly, a community awareness app focused on "Know Before You Go." Beta testers can check local reports, review the map, and share feedback about clarity, trust, and usefulness. Gridly is in beta and is not for emergencies. For emergencies, always call 911 or local emergency services.
