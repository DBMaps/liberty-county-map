# Gridly Beta Known Limitations

Gridly is in beta. Some parts of the experience may change as feedback is reviewed and the product improves.

## Community Reports

Reports depend on community activity. Some areas may have few reports, and a quiet map does not always mean conditions are clear.

## Changing Conditions

Conditions can change quickly. Always use your own judgment, obey traffic laws, and follow official instructions when they are available.

## Emergencies

Gridly is not for emergencies. Do not use Gridly as an emergency reporting tool. For emergencies, always call 911 or local emergency services.

## Notifications

Notifications are not fully available yet unless already supported in the current build on your device and browser. During beta, open Gridly directly when you want to check conditions.

## Feature Changes

Some features, wording, and layouts may change during beta. Changes are expected as tester feedback helps improve clarity and confidence.
