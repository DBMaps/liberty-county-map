# Gridly Mobile Tester Checklist

## Purpose

Every Gridly mobile beta tester should complete this checklist on a phone in portrait orientation. Estimated completion time: **15 to 20 minutes**.

## Checklist

- [ ] Open the current beta URL on a phone in portrait orientation.
- [ ] Install Gridly to the home screen if you are comfortable doing so.
- [ ] Launch Gridly from the browser or home-screen icon.
- [ ] Complete onboarding.
- [ ] Choose or confirm your awareness area.
- [ ] Grant location if comfortable, or decline and note whether the next step is clear.
- [ ] Verify the map loads.
- [ ] Review the Awareness Brief.
- [ ] Submit one report only if it is safe and appropriate.
- [ ] Review Community Pulse.
- [ ] Visit Settings.
- [ ] Verify notification and settings behavior is understandable on your device.
- [ ] Send one piece of feedback about what was clear, useful, confusing, or broken.

## Feedback Prompt

Please include:

- Device and browser.
- Whether you installed Gridly or used the browser.
- Whether onboarding was clear.
- Whether the map and Awareness Brief made sense.
- Whether Settings and notification language were understandable.
- One thing that would make Gridly easier to trust or use.

## Completion Estimate

Most testers should complete the checklist in **15 to 20 minutes**. Longer times are useful feedback if install, onboarding, location, map loading, reports, Community Pulse, or Settings take extra effort.
