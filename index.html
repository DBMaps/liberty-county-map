    About Gridly
    Mission

    GRIDLY

    Know Before You Go

    Gridly helps communities share awareness about blocked crossings, road hazards, flooding, closures, and changing travel conditions before they affect the day.

    Community Commitment

    Our Commitment

    Gridly was built for residents, commuters, schools, businesses, and first responders.

    A portion of future proceeds will be reinvested into the communities we serve.

    Public safety partnerships remain a priority.

    Dispatch and first responder use will always remain free.

    Beta Notice

    Beta Notice

    Gridly is currently in beta.

    Features, reports, and community activity may evolve as the platform continues to improve.

    Important Safety Notice

    Important

    Gridly is a community awareness platform.

    Gridly should not be used to report emergencies.

    For emergencies, always call 911 or contact local emergency services.

    Always use your own judgment and obey traffic laws.

    How Trust Works

    How Trust Works

    Gridly uses community reports, confirmations, and recent observations to help people know before they go.

    Community reports are awareness signals, not guarantees.

    Conditions can change quickly.
